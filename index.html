How long does shipping take? ▼
We ship within 1–2 business days of your order. Shipping is 2–3 day priority and is already included in your setup fee — no extra charges.

Your monthly plan covers your streaming service access, all app updates, and ongoing technical support. If anything ever stops working, call or text us and we'll fix it.

Yes, absolutely. There are no contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time directly through your account.
